Who We Are

Three Founders.
Three Domains.
One Vision.

Most AI companies are built by engineers alone. SyriLabs is different. We were founded in Miami by a physician, an MIT-trained engineer, and a marketing strategist — because the best AI isn't just technically sound, it's built by people who deeply understand the problems it solves.

Our founding thesis is simple: AI solutions fail when they're designed in isolation from the industries they serve. By embedding domain expertise into every layer — from data collection and model training to deployment and optimization — we build systems that clinicians actually trust, platforms that engineers can scale, and strategies that marketers can measure.

Headquartered in Miami's rapidly growing tech ecosystem, we serve clients across the Americas and beyond, bringing a unique blend of Latin American market insight and world-class technical capability to every engagement.

Industry-First Approach

We start with deep domain knowledge, not generic algorithms. Our physician validates every healthcare model against real clinical workflows. Our strategist pressure-tests every marketing model against real campaign data. Every model is trained, validated, and deployed by people who've worked in the industry it serves — not just engineers who read about it.

02

Production-Ready from Day One

No demos, no POCs gathering dust. Our MIT-trained engineering team builds for scale, security, and reliability from the first line of code. Every system includes monitoring, alerting, graceful failure modes, and rollback capabilities. We don't ship experiments — we ship products.

03

Measurable Impact

Every engagement is tied to clear KPIs agreed upon before we write a single line of code. We measure success by your outcomes — revenue lifted, costs reduced, time saved, accuracy improved — not by how sophisticated our models are. If we can't define the metric, we don't start the project.

04

Cross-Disciplinary Teams

Clinicians, engineers, and strategists working together on every project. This isn't a staffing agency model — it's a deeply integrated team where the doctor challenges the engineer's assumptions and the strategist holds both accountable to business results.

05

Ethical & Responsible AI

We build AI with transparency, fairness, and accountability baked into every system. That means bias audits, explainability layers, data privacy by design, and clear documentation of model limitations. Our physician co-founder brings a "first, do no harm" mindset to everything we ship.

06

Long-Term Partnership, Not One-Off Projects

AI isn't a one-and-done deliverable. Models drift, data changes, and business needs evolve. We build relationships, not just systems — providing ongoing optimization, retraining, and strategic guidance to ensure your AI investment compounds over time. Most of our clients have been with us since our earliest days, and that's by design.

How We Work

From Idea to
Production

Our battle-tested process ensures every project moves from concept to deployed solution with speed, clarity, and zero surprises.

01

Discovery

We spend 1-2 weeks diving deep into your business, workflows, data landscape, and strategic goals. We interview stakeholders, audit existing data, and identify the highest-impact AI opportunities. You'll walk away with a clear roadmap — even if you decide not to work with us.

02

Design

Our cross-functional team architects a solution tailored to your domain, data, and success metrics. We define the model architecture, data pipeline, integration points, and deployment strategy. You'll see wireframes, system diagrams, and a detailed project plan before we write any code.

03

Develop

Iterative builds in 2-week sprints with continuous validation from domain experts. You'll see working demos every two weeks, with full transparency into model performance, edge cases, and remaining risks. No black boxes, no surprises — just steady, measurable progress.

04

Deploy & Scale

Production deployment with comprehensive monitoring, automated alerting, performance dashboards, and ongoing optimization. We don't hand off and disappear. We provide ongoing support, model retraining, and strategic guidance to ensure your system gets better over time.
